EDOSA GAXKIN OVIAWE

Edosa Oviawe is a professional gender and reproductive health rights advocate with extensive knowledge in programming in gender governance and reproductive rights. He is the Board Chair of the organization.

ONUH OJONUGWA DAVIDSON

Onuh Ojonugwa Davidson is a youth development and reproductive health expert with over eight years experience working and volunteering for different NGOs in Nigeria advocating for youth issues around sexual reproductive health & HIV/AIDS, human rights, governance, gender justice for women and young girls in Nigeria. He is a graduate of Biochemistry with from Kogi State University, Anyigba - Nigeria. He is skilled in organizing campaigns, project planning & management, technology and communications, filming & photography, and also delights in creating partnership and collaborations. Works as the technical lead on all technology for health related projects, as well as coordinate the development and management of all technology tools and applications for health programs. He is a reproductive health and rights issues counselor as well. Onuh possesses strong background experience in program design, planning and implementation especially adolescents sexual reproductive health and rights related programs.

MUSA OKINAYI MARGARET

Margaret is a lawyer, called to the Nigerian bar in 2015. She is a certified peer health educator and an ICT Law enthusiast. She is the legal adviser to the organization.

ONUKWUGHA FRANKLIN

Franklin Onukwugha is a lecturer in Public Health at Coventry University UK and an associate lecturer at the University of Sunderland United Kingdom. He is a Fellow of the Royal Society for Public Health (FRSPH) UK and a member of several professional associations such as British Society for Population Studies (BSPS), International Union of the Scientific Study of Population (IUSSP), Population Association of America (PAA). He is a recipient of several prestigious fellowships including the Population Reference Bureau Policy Communication Fellow sponsored by the United State Agency for International Development (USAID) in partnership with the African Institute for Development Policy.

DIJI ATINUKE

Atinuke Diji is a Medical Scientist and graduate of the prestigious University of Edinburgh, Edinburg. She is a Board member of the organization.
